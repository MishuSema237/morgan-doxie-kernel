About This Puppy

Meet Max, a stunning 8-week-old Golden Retriever male with an exceptional temperament. Max comes from champion bloodlines with both parents health tested and certified. His gentle nature and playful personality make him the perfect family companion.

Max is already showing signs of high intelligence and eagerness to learn. He responds well to his name and has begun basic house training. His socialization has been a priority from day one, with exposure to various sounds, surfaces, and gentle handling by adults and children.

His energy level is moderate to high, typical of the Golden Retriever breed. Max loves playtime but also enjoys quiet cuddles. He gets along wonderfully with other dogs and has been raised with children, making him an ideal choice for families.

Max has a beautiful golden coat that is soft and fluffy. Regular grooming will be needed as he grows. His structure is excellent with good angulation and a level topline, indicating he will mature into a handsome, well-built adult dog.

He has been fed premium puppy food and his growth has been monitored weekly. Max is at the perfect weight for his age and shows no signs of any health concerns. His eyes are clear, ears clean, and teeth healthy.

This puppy has been raised in a home environment with lots of love and attention. He is comfortable with household sounds including vacuum cleaners, television, and doorbells. Early neurological stimulation exercises were performed during his critical development period.

Max will come with a comprehensive puppy pack including his health certificate, vaccination records, microchip information, feeding schedule, and a blanket with familiar scents from his mother and littermates. We also provide lifetime breeder support for any questions or concerns.

Ideal home: Active family with children or individuals who enjoy outdoor activities. Golden Retrievers thrive with regular exercise and mental stimulation. A secure yard is recommended though not required. Max will need ongoing training and socialization to become the best companion possible.
