Our Breeding Philosophy

Breeder with Puppies

I started breeding dogs over 5 years ago out of my deep love for these amazing animals. What began as a passion project has grown into a trusted breeding business serving families across Lagos and beyond.

My approach to breeding is simple but unwavering: every puppy deserves the best possible start in life. I raise all puppies in my home environment, ensuring they receive constant love, attention, and proper socialization from the moment they're born.

Each puppy is thoroughly vet-checked, vaccinated, and comes with complete health records. I take immense pride in matching the right puppy with the right family, and I'm committed to providing lifetime support to ensure every dog thrives.

Over the years, I've had the incredible joy of placing 50+ puppies with loving families throughout Nigeria. Seeing these dogs grow up healthy, happy, and deeply bonded with their families is what makes this work so profoundly rewarding.

I believe in transparency and building trust. That's why I welcome visits to see where the puppies are raised, meet the parent dogs, and ask any questions. When you adopt from me, you're not just getting a puppy - you're joining our extended family.

My commitment goes beyond the sale. I'm here for advice, support, and guidance throughout your dog's entire life. Whether you need training tips, health advice, or just want to share a photo, I'm always just a message away.

Frequently Asked Questions

What health guarantees do you provide?
All puppies come with a comprehensive 30-day health guarantee, complete vet health certificate, full vaccination records, and deworming history. We stand behind the health of every puppy we place. If any genetic health issues arise within the first year, we work with you to resolve them.
Can I visit the puppies before purchasing?
Yes! We welcome and strongly encourage visits by appointment. You can meet the puppy, see where they're raised, meet the parent dogs, and ask any questions. We believe transparency builds trust. Please call or WhatsApp ahead to schedule your visit.
What's included in the purchase price?
Purchase includes: official vet health certificate, age-appropriate vaccinations, deworming treatment, starter food supply, favorite toy, blanket with mother's scent, care guide, and lifetime support from us. Microchipping is available for an additional fee.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, we offer flexible installment plans. A 50% deposit is required to reserve your chosen puppy, with the remaining balance due before pickup. We want to make it easier for families to bring home their perfect companion. Contact us to discuss terms.
What if I need to return the puppy?
We have a return policy if circumstances change. The deposit is non-refundable but can be transferred to another puppy within 6 months. We always prioritize the welfare of our dogs and will work with you to find the best solution if issues arise.
Do you ship puppies outside Lagos?
We prefer local pickup for the puppy's comfort and your peace of mind, but we can arrange safe delivery within Nigeria for an additional fee. We ensure proper transport with climate control, frequent breaks, and all necessary documentation. International shipping is not available.
What ongoing support do you provide?
Lifetime support! I'm always available via WhatsApp, phone, or email for questions about training, health, nutrition, behavior, or anything else. You're part of our extended family. Many of my clients stay in touch for years, sending updates and photos. I love seeing how the puppies grow!
How do you socialize the puppies?
Puppies are raised in my home with daily human interaction from birth. They're exposed to household sounds, different people, gentle handling, and positive experiences. Early socialization continues with introductions to various stimuli, sounds, and situations to ensure confident, well-adjusted puppies.
What breeds do you specialize in?
We specialize in Golden Retrievers, German Shepherds, Labrador Retrievers, Rottweilers, French Bulldogs, and American Bullies. Each breed is carefully selected for temperament, health, and family compatibility. Visit our Breeds page to learn more about each one.
At what age can puppies go to new homes?
Puppies go to their new homes at a minimum of 8 weeks old, ensuring they've had crucial time with their mother and littermates for proper social development. Some breeds may stay slightly longer depending on individual development and breed-specific needs.
